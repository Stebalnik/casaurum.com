- `imageAssets`
- `videoAssets`
- `schemaData`
- `internalLinks`
- `leadFormType`
- `qualityScore`
- `indexingStatus`
- `lastUpdated`

Indexing statuses:

- `draft`
- `needs_review`
- `noindex`
- `indexable`
- `approved`

Only generated pages that are approved and pass the quality gate are included in `sitemap.xml`. Review pages can render as `noindex,follow` so they can be inspected without being pushed into search.

Quality gate:

- unique search intent
- unique SEO title
- unique meta description
- unique H1
- at least 800 words of useful localized content
- not a city-name swap
- relevant visible FAQ
- internal links to parent vertical, service, location and CTA pages
- honest local wording
- no fake offices, crews, licenses, projects, testimonials, awards or showrooms
- relevant image metadata
- localized alt text and captions
- hreflang alternates
- visible-content-matching schema
- clear conversion path

## Local Wording Rules

Georgia:

CAS AURUM accepts kitchen, cabinetry, wall panel, furniture and millwork inquiries across Georgia, with priority service available in Atlanta and surrounding areas.

Other markets:

CAS AURUM provides design direction, custom interior concepts, measurement requests, material specifications and project coordination. For production and installation outside the immediate service area, CAS AURUM may coordinate with vetted local professionals and regional production partners.

Never claim:

- physical offices in every city
- local crews in every city
- licensed contractor status in every state
- completed projects in every city
- same-day remodeling everywhere
- fake reviews
- fake showrooms
- fake awards

## Content Rules

- Write for real homeowners, designers, architects, builders, developers and hospitality operators.
- Avoid keyword stuffing and thin doorway pages.
- Do not claim fake offices, fake completed projects, fake clients, fake awards or fake testimonials.
- Use regional pages to describe service availability, not physical office presence.
- Future city pages should be data-driven and enriched with useful local project context before publishing.
