## Priority Order

1. Interior designers with luxury residential positioning.
2. Custom home builders in Buckhead, Brookhaven, Sandy Springs, Alpharetta and Atlanta ITP.
3. Kitchen and bath firms handling custom cabinetry/remodel scopes.
4. Architects and interior architecture studios.
5. Hospitality / restaurant designers after the residential list is contacted.

## Follow-Up Sequence

Day 0:
- Send short email or contact form message.
- Add status: contacted.

Day 2:
- Follow up with one collection link.
- Recommended angle: Aurum for quiet luxury, Madera for wood/refined residential, Forma for modern geometry.

Day 7:
- Ask one simple question: “Do you ever need custom panels, closets, built-ins or cabinetry support for client projects?”

Day 14:
- Final soft follow-up with no pressure: “I’ll close the loop here, but happy to review drawings or inspiration if a project comes up.”
